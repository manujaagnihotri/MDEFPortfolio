Elevator Pitch

What is it? 
- An elevator pitch is a concise and compelling summary of an idea, product, or project that can be delivered in the time it takes to ride an elevator, typically around 30 seconds to 2 minutes. It's meant to quickly grab someone's attention and convey the most important aspects or benefits of whatever you're pitching. The goal is to leave a memorable impression and ideally spark further interest or conversation.
