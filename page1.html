About Me

Welcome to my design journey into the future! I'm a 22-year-old passionate design enthusiast hailing from the vibrant and culturally rich land of India and China. Armed with a bachelor's degree in Industrial Design, I am now on an exciting expedition through the realm of emerging future design.

Currently, I am immersed in pursuing my master's degree, focusing on harnessing the power of emergent technologies to craft sustainable solutions and advocate for inclusivity in design. 

Through this website, I invite you to join me on this exhilarating ride as I document my experiences, insights, and growth in the realm of emergent future design.
