Learning about NFTs, Blockchains and DAOs.

Designing with collective intelligence

1. NFTs (Non-Fungible Tokens): Digital assets that represent ownership or proof of authenticity of unique items or content, often using blockchain technology to verify their uniqueness and ownership.

2. Blockchains: A decentralized, distributed ledger technology that records transactions across multiple computers in a way that is immutable, transparent, and secure. It enables the creation of NFTs and cryptocurrencies like Bitcoin and Ethereum.

3. DAOs (Decentralized Autonomous Organizations): Organizations that are governed by smart contracts and operate without centralized control. They allow participants to vote on decisions and manage resources transparently through blockchain technology, aiming for decentralized governance and decision-making.

Having lectures on these complex terms and technologies was quite eye-opening and especially to see the possibilities of intertwining communities to create safe networks. Upon understanding the very basics of blockchains and DAOs, we voted on our topics of interest areas of issues. After having our teams made, we researched slightly on our topics and found resources and references that support the topic.
