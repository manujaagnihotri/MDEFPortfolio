LANDING WEEK

A warm welcome to Barcelona and IAAC.

Day 1 & 2

The first week of my Master's program in Emergent Future Design at the Institute for Advanced Architecture of Catalonia (IAAC) was an enlightening and immersive experience as it set the tone for what promises to be an exciting and transformative academic journey.

Landing week began with a warm welcome with an introductory session that provided a comprehensive overview of the course expectations and a detailed term-wise plan for the entire year. This initial briefing was essential in helping me grasp the scope and structure of the course, enabling me to align my expectations and goals accordingly. 

Day 3 & 4

Documenting Design was a particularly enlightening session, focusing on the crucial aspect of documentation. We delved into understanding how to effectively document our work, an essential skill in the field of emergent future design. Learning from the experiences and work of senior students was invaluable, providing insights into various documentation styles and methods. Understanding the importance of clear and comprehensive documentation in conveying ideas and progress became evident, emphasizing its role in facilitating collaboration and knowledge sharing within the academic community.

During this day, we also delved into the world of GitHub and the terminal, gaining proficiency in these tools for version control and collaborative development. Learning how to launch our websites using these platforms was a pivotal moment, as it set the stage for us to showcase our work to a broader audience, enhancing our digital presence and sharing our creations with the world. 

DAY 5

On the last day of this week, we were introduced to the Elisava Design School campus, an exciting opportunity to familiarize ourselves with the environment in which we'll be honing our skills. Understanding the workshop machinery available to us was enlightening, as it offered insights into the tools and resources at our disposal to bring our design concepts to life. Being in a creative and equipped space like Elisava instilled a sense of excitement and motivation, highlighting the potential for hands-on experimentation and innovation.

Overall, this first week has been a whirlwind of information, introductions, and inspiration. It has provided a solid foundation for what lies ahead, offering a glimpse into the rich learning experiences and opportunities for growth that the Emergent Future Design program at IAAC holds. I look forward to immersing myself further in this journey, embracing the challenges and embracing the innovations that await in the weeks and months to come.
