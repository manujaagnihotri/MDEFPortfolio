DESIGN DIALOGUES

Design dialogues was the most perfect ending to the term to showcase our work and achievements. Having the privilege to explain our hard work and receive feedback was a fun experience. Power Play was one of the projects that I had on display that allowed me to explain the science behind the animation and the positive analysis of gathering data. The feedback that I received from the faculty was helpful to sculpt my future projects and to get a clear insight of my area of insight, especially because I am slightly confused and unsure of which area to focus on entirely. Hopefully, in the upcoming terms I can work on my projects that focus on more solarpunk, smart city and human machine collaboration.
