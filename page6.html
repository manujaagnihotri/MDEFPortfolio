About Me

Welcome to my design journey into the future! I'm a 22-year-old passionate design enthusiast hailing from the vibrant and culturally rich land of India and China. Armed with a bachelor's degree in Industrial Design, I am now on an exciting expedition through the realm of emerging future design.

Currently, I am immersed in pursuing my master's degree, focusing on harnessing the power of emergent technologies to craft sustainable solutions and advocate for inclusivity in design. My enthusiasm for thorough research fuels my quest to understand, adapt, and utilize the cutting-edge technologies that are shaping the world of tomorrow.

Through this website, I invite you to join me on this exhilarating ride as I document my experiences, insights, and growth in the realm of emergent future design. Let's collaborate, learn, and co-create a future where sustainable and inclusive design is at the core of our endeavors. Together, we'll envision and shape a better tomorrow, today.
